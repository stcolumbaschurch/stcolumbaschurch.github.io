Music

Have a listen to the music for Sunday's Mass so even at home, you can sing along!

Below is a playlist of videos of the hymns which will be included in Sunday's Mass.
We have put them here so that you can listen to the music in advance of Live Streamed Mass so that you can be familiar with the hymns and maybe even sing along at home!
Details on how to use
  • The videos are ordered in the order of Mass
  • You can watch all of the videos in order just by pressing the play button - the next video will play automatically
  • To skip videos or go to a specific video, press the playlist button (3 horizontal lines stacked on top of each other with a small triangle in the bottom right corner) which will bring up a menu where you can select a specific song's video
