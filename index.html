Welcome to St Columba's

If you’re looking for a friendly and active Catholic community in Chesham, then you’ve come to the right place at St Columba’s.
You’ll find us at the north end of Chesham on the Berkhampstead Road at the bottom of Nashleigh Hill and you are very welcome to join us at our Sunday Mass

 We want it to be as easy as possible for you to get the information you need. That is why we have updated our grid of Quick Links to include more pages so that you can get to where you need to be quickly.
