A message from Fr Alessandro

To my parishioner family,

After this latest update that follows the guidelines of the Government of our country, the risk is that we will feel deeply abandoned... Especially as the doors of our places of prayer, where we went to seek peace and consolation in the last days, have also been shut. But it is not so: only the buildings have been closed; we are living Churches and as far we keep remain hope through the prayer at home, keeping the hope alive and trusting in the Lord... Churches will never close. The Lord promised his disciples that he would never leave them alone: in every situation of life he would be close to them by sending the Comforter Spirit who would help, support and comfort them.

We will increase our live streaming daily and I will celebrate the Eucharist for each one of you everyday. Very soon we will update websites and Facebook pages so you can see what is happen every day!

We are family: we will never be alone.
God Bless you all
In fiducia cordis,

Fr Alessandro Renò
Effective immediately:
  • All public Masses at St Columba's are suspended from Friday 20th March 2020
  • Fr Alessandro will live stream his private celebration of Mass on Sunday at 10am via YouTube - Click Here to see IHM and St Columba's joint YouTube channel
  • In line with the latest Government instruction our church building will remain closed.
