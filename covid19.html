Update Opening of Churches

One Mass will be celebrated each Sunday in August on alternate weeks as planned but Fr Alessandro will now be inside the Church with those parishioners who are involved in the streaming and the liturgy only. The Mass will continue to be live-streamed on the YouTube channel and will also be broadcasted simultaneously by speaker in the church grounds.
Parishioners can stand in the grounds to listen to the broadcast & have visuals on their own phones or tablets. Holy Communion will be distributed to parishioners outside the Church, with the Stewards helping with logistics.
The Church grounds/area will be marked up to meet social-distancing requirements and this will unfortunately limit numbers.


As numbers are limited due to social distancing, if you wish to attend Mass in the grounds please let us know by e-mail to massbookingatihmandstc@gmail.com or telephone the parish secretary.
We want to trial this “booking” process for when we return to public Mass inside the churches in September when numbers will continue to be limited due to social distancing requirements.

The first Mass will be celebrated at IHM on 2 August at 10.00 am, this will be followed by:

  • 9 August at St Columba’s at 10.00 am
  • 16 August at IHM at 10.00 am
  • 23 August at St Columba’s at 10.00 am
  • 30 August at IHM at 10.00 am

The details for these outdoor Masses can be found on the Public Calendar which can be found on the Event Page.

Thank you for your patience and understanding as we try to get back to a “new normal” for public Mass and participate together as a community. From August we will have two ways to participate – either in the limited numbers in the Church grounds or at home via streaming.
