Youth Music Group at St Columba's

Our parish Youth MUSIC GROUP (band & amp; choir) bring vibrant energy and heartfelt worship to the 6pm Sunday Mass each week, creating a joyful and uplifting atmosphere for the congregation. Once a month, we also lead a Youth led Mass with a selection of contemporary Christian worship music, which attracts many families, children and young people.
In preparation, the group gathers the day before, on Saturday, from 5 to 6pm for music practice, followed by a fun-filled evening of pizza, games, and creative arts and crafts inspired by the liturgical season (until 7pm). These sessions not only strengthen our bond as a music group but also help us grow in faith and friendship as we serve together our parish community with our God-given talents. New members are always welcome!
If you wish to find out more, please contact Federica: federica@olschesham.co.uk
